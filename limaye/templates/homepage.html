What We Do

The Subject of scientific enquiry in our laboratory stands at the crossroad where multiple disciplines meet - molecular endorinology, cancer biology and reproductive biology. The focus is on understanding the mechanism underlying the development and progression of endocrine cancers. We also study hormonal regulation of genes. We use molecular biology tools with cell culture and animal models for our investigations.

The idea is to try and answer small questions that interrogate the "parts" in order to understand the "whole".
